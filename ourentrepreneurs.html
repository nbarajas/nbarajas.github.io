Nate Barajas, Kellogg School of Management, Class of 2018

Mr. Barajas is a former private equity professional who has over six years of experience working directly with the senior leadership teams of small businesses in a variety of industries. Mr. Barajas chose to attend Kellogg to learn how to better manage teams, develop employees, and enhance his emotional intelligence. Mr. Barajas seeks an apprenticeship opportunity, partnering with a small business executive who would be eager to share his or her experience and seeks assistance in removing growth constraints on his or her business. In his free time, Mr. Barajas enjoys playing tennis, watching college sports, and spending time with his wife Kristen, and his english bulldog, Porter.
